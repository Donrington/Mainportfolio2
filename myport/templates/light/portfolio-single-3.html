Project Description

E-Book is a cutting-edge e-commerce platform designed to cater to the diverse preferences of book lovers. Developed using React with Vite, this website offers an expansive collection of both physical books and e-books across various genres. With a focus on user experience and efficient performance, E-Book provides a seamless shopping experience for readers looking to explore new titles or purchase their favorite books online.
