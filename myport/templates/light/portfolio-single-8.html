Welcome to the GPS Location Tracker, an advanced web application developed using Python Flask and Bootstrap. This platform allows users to track their live location and view their location history with ease and precision. Whether you're navigating a new city or keeping tabs on your daily routes, our GPS tracker ensures you always know where you are and where you've been.

Our GPS Location Tracker is designed to provide a seamless user experience, combining real-time location tracking with a robust history feature. Users can register and log in to view their movement history, add notes to specific locations, and even set up notifications for specific areas. With an intuitive interface and reliable performance, our tracker is the perfect tool for anyone looking to stay on top of their movements and travel patterns.
